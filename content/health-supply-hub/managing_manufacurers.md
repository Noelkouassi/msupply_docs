### Manufacturers

Identifying the correct final dose form manufacturer is an important step to ensure customers understand the product being supplied.
To make this easier for suppliers, the Health Supply Hub allows suppliers to maintain their own list of manufacturers. This list will be mapping to a master list before Purchase orders are created.
